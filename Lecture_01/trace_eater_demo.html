Trace Eater

A retro Pac-Man-style metaphor for digital methods: collect likes, comments, emojis, hashtags, and links, then convert traces into interpretation.

Score: 0
Lives: 3
Level: Collection
Traces left: 0

How To Play

Use arrow keys or WASD. Collect traces and avoid noise agents. Hashtags power you up briefly so you can clear noise instead of getting trapped by it.

Teaching Point

Collection mode: gather traces. The score goes up, but the meaning is still thin until you start reading patterns.

Why It Fits Slide 1

  • You collect digital traces instead of ordinary pellets.
  • The maze turns platform activity into visible research material.
  • The mode shift from collection to interpretation illustrates the core idea of digital methods.
